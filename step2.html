Common questions

Quick answers

Do I need to be in shape to start? +

Nope. Xplicit is great for all levels. Each person gets their own station and works at their own pace. Take breaks when you need, we help you modify, and you progress at your own pace. Total beginners train next to 15-year members in the same class.

I’m a busy parent. Will I have time? +

Our classes are 40 minutes. You get strength training with weights, cardio with boxing, mobility, and core work in one shot. In, out, and on with your day. We have classes from 5am to 6:30pm M–F plus Saturday mornings.

What if I feel embarrassed or out of place? +

We welcome everyone. Some of our members have been with us for 15 years — for others it’s day one. Whether you’re pro level or starting an exercise program for the first time, we meet you right where you’re at. No judgment, just coaching.

Is the $29 really all I pay? +

Yes. $29 gets you 10 classes — that’s it. No autopay. No automatic renewal. After your 10 classes, you can choose to join a membership (most people do, but you’re never required to). The intro is the intro.

How long do I have to use my 10 classes? +

You have 2 weeks from your first visit to use all 10 classes — that’s about 5 classes per week. We designed it this way on purpose: two weeks of consistent training is what produces real results, builds the habit, and gives you a true sense of whether Xplicit is right for you long-term. Your coach will help you map out a schedule that fits your life.

What’s your cancellation policy? +

We ask for 5 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a class. No-shows and late cancels are $20. We keep this policy because every class has limited spots — missing without notice means another member couldn’t take that spot. Easy to cancel through the app or by texting your coach.

What do I need to bring? +

Just yourself, water, and workout clothes you can move in. You can bring your own boxing gloves and wraps, or rent/buy them from us. Show up 10 minutes early for your first class so a coach can get you set up. Free parking on-site.
